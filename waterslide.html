About Our Waterslide

Transform your backyard into an exciting water park with our thrilling inflatable waterslide! This vibrant water attraction features a main slide, splash pool, and unique aqua cave for engaging playtime. Perfect for summer events and guaranteed hours of fun!

Features:

  • Main slide with refreshing splash pool
  • Unique aqua cave for interactive play
  • Challenging climb wall
  • Water play fountain
  • Integrated water cannon
  • High-quality, durable materials
  • Quick and easy setup in minutes
  • Specifications: 14.8 feet long x 12 feet wide x 8 feet high (54.7 lbs)
  • Suitable for ages 5+ (children under 12 require adult supervision)

Perfect for: Summer birthday parties, family gatherings, neighborhood events, school picnics, and any outdoor celebration!

⚠️ Important: Waterslide requires a water source and adequate outdoor space for setup. Please ensure your venue meets these requirements when booking.
