About Our Waterslide

Cool off and have a blast with our fantastic waterslide! This exciting inflatable water attraction is the perfect centerpiece for summer events and guarantees hours of fun for guests of all ages.

Features:

  • 20' long sliding surface with splash pool
  • Colorful, eye-catching design
  • High-capacity water circulation system
  • Safe, easy-to-climb stairs
  • Built-in pool area at the bottom
  • Requires outdoor space and water source
  • Sanitized and chlorinated water system
  • Suitable for ages 5+ (children under 12 require adult supervision)

Perfect for: Summer birthday parties, family gatherings, neighborhood events, school picnics, neighborhood gatherings, and any outdoor celebration!

⚠️ Important: Waterslide requires a water source and at least 20' x 30' of outdoor space. Please ensure your venue meets these requirements when booking.
